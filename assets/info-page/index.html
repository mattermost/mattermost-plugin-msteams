How will you be using the two platforms?
Mattermost will be my primary platform
What this choice means:
  • Linked channel and direct messages from Microsoft Teams will be synced in Mattermost
  • You will get notifications in Mattermost for synced messages and channels.
  • You will need to disable notifications in Microsoft Teams to avoid duplicate notifications or emails. Learn more
Microsoft Teams will be my primary platform
What this choice means:
  • Linked channel and direct messages from Microsoft Teams will still be synced in Mattermost
  • Notifications in Mattermost will be disabled for messages coming from Microsoft Teams to prevent duplicates. Learn more
  • Unread status in synced channels and direct messages will be disabled in Mattermost.
Let's go!
You can change this setting in your account setting later. For more information visit this help article.
